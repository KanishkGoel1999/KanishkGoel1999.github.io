About Me

Let me take you through my educational pursuits, technical skills, and notable experiences.

My academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation in data-related disciplines, including data warehousing, data mining and machine learning. During my undergraduate years, I delved into subjects like statistics and probability which laid the groundwork for my analytical prowess.

I'm excited to explore new data-driven challenges, collaborate on innovative projects, and continuously enhance my skill set.
